# Business Intelligence and KPI Systems

**Type:** Skill category  
**Category:** Technical — Analytics

## What this covers

Building BI ecosystems that give executives actionable visibility, not just data. The difference between a dashboard that requires interpretation and one that surfaces what needs attention.

## Specific capabilities

### Executive Dashboards
- Built Power BI dashboards for Dell Technologies Server Revenue Operations ($16B+ scope)
- Integrated pricing, pipeline, and operational data into unified executive views
- Designed KPI systems used across the revenue operations organization

### Operational Analytics
- Built dashboards at ACHS hospital that reduced reporting effort by **90%**
- Automated weekly reporting cycles that previously consumed significant analyst time
- Created real-time operational views for healthcare service delivery

### BI Ecosystem Design
- Designed decision ecosystems that connected multiple data sources into unified systems
- Built SQL operating frameworks for revenue planning
- Created automated data pipelines from multiple business systems
