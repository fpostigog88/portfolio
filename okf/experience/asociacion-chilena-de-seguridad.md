# Asociacion Chilena de Seguridad — Manager Commercial Strategy and Operations

**Role:** Manager Commercial Strategy and Operations  
**Period:** 2020–2021  
**Organization:** Asociacion Chilena de Seguridad (ACHS) — Hospital  
**Location:** Chile

## What this role involved

Redesigned commercial strategy, customer engagement processes, and internal operations for a Chilean hospital network. The core problem was a disconnected sales process and a reporting layer that consumed enormous analyst time without producing actionable insight.

## Key accomplishments

- **20% increase in sales** through redesigned commercial workflows
- **60% acceleration** in invoicing and service delivery efficiency
- **90% reduction** in reporting effort through automated dashboards
- Built a full **operational analytics** system replacing manual report generation
- Redesigned **data-driven commercial workflows** across the organization

## Technical approach

- Built operational dashboards that replaced weekly manual reporting cycles
- Designed automated data pipelines from multiple hospital systems
- Created KPIs tracked in real-time by leadership
- Mapped and redesigned customer engagement processes to eliminate bottlenecks
